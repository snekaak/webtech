ABOUT US

SHIYA Pets SHOP is one of the leading pet shops in Coimbatore. The shop is located in Coimbatore, India. They have dogs, birds, cats and aquarium fishes (Marine and Freshwater) of quality breeds in excellent health condition and temperament. All their pets are handpicked with excellent quality and good lineage. They sell dogs like pugs, Dobermans, rottweilers, great dane, boxers, golden retrievers, rajapalyam, chipiparai, kombai, dachshunds, beagles, labrador retrievers, german shepherds, chihuahua and other exotic dogs as mentioned in their Dogs section based on the order. They sell birds like cockatoos, macaws, African greys, conures, amazons, African birds and other exotic birds on an order basis.
